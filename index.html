Our Story

A taste of home,
served with pride

Spice Village became one of South London's most iconic food spots — a place where rich karahis, sizzling BBQ, and slow-cooked family recipes bring the flavour of Lahore's streets to Tooting.

Every dish is prepared fresh to order, using time-tested spice blends and recipes passed down through generations. From weekend Nihari to Sunday Lahori Nashta, this is Pakistani hospitality in its truest form.
